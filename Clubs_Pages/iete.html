About IETE Club

An IETE Club, or IETE Student Forum (ISF), is a student body under the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE), a professional organization in India focused on advancing electronics, telecommunication, IT, and computer science. ISFs organize technical activities, workshops, and competitions to foster skill development in students.
