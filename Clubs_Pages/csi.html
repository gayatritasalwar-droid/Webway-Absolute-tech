About CSI

The Computer Society of India (CSI) is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. Our student chapter aims to facilitate research, knowledge sharing, learning and career enhancement for students while also inspiring and nurturing new entrants into the IT community.

We provide a platform for students to interact with industry experts, participate in technical workshops, coding competitions, and hackathons. Our members get opportunities to work on real-world projects and develop skills that are highly valued in the IT industry.
